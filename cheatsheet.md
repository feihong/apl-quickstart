# Cheatsheet

## Functions

### Conjugate

    +56  -->  56
    +4j3  -->  (4-3j)
    +4j¯3  -->  (4+3j)

### Plus

    1 2 3+4 5 6  -->  [5, 7, 9]

### Negate

    -55  -->  -55

### Minus

    1 2 3 4-100  -->  [-99, -98, -97, -96]

### Reshape

    2 2⍴1 2 3 4  -->  [[1, 2], [3, 4]]
